Buyers and Sellers

Buyers

Let us assist you with the purchase of your property. We offer a wide range of services to protect your interests from the time of the offer through the closing of the purchase. For buyers who are not utilizing the services of a real estate broker, we can assist you with the preparation of a purchase and sale agreement. Additionally, we will assist your lender by providing a timely title examination and preparation of closing documents. If you are not working with a lender, we offer these services directly to you. Consumer Title will negotiate the complicated waters of real estate conveyancing on your behalf and assure that your interests are protected throughout the transaction. And after the sale, our title insurance policies will protect your interest in your new property.
