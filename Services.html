Services

Consumer Title offers a full complement of residential and small business, title and closing services. We look forward to assisting you with your closing document needs, from Hud-1 preparation to complete closing documentation. Our signing services are available at our office, yours, or any other location convenient to you or your customer.

We perform title examinations throughout the State of Maine and offer title insurance to you and your customers. Also offered is a range of other services such as: current owner searches; mortgage discharges and releases; subordination agreements; construction run downs; and just about anything else you may need to support your loan. We can also prepare your note, mortgage and other documents you may require for your loan. Let us know how we can help.
